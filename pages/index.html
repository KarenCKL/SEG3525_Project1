About Us

Ottawa Dental Clinic opened its doors in 2014. Since then, we have worked hard to provide a comprehensive and holistic treatment approach to all of our patients. Our dental clinic is located at 150 Louis Pasteur, Ottawa. We have assembled the best team of dentists, specialists, dental hygienists, and support personnel.

Ottawa Dental Clinic is dedicated to providing comprehensive, expert care. Every smile we treat has the potential to achieve long-term oral health and unparalleled beauty! We excel at excellent dentistry at Ottawa Dental Care by going above and beyond the typical neighbourhood dentist.
