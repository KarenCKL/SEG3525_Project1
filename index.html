About Ottawa Dental Clinic

Ottawa Dental Clinic has a rich history that dates back to its establishment in 2014. Since its inception, the clinic has been dedicated to providing comprehensive and holistic dental care to patients in Ottawa and the surrounding areas. With a focus on excellence and a commitment to patient satisfaction, Ottawa Dental Clinic quickly gained a reputation as a trusted dental practice in the community.

Over the years, Ottawa Dental Clinic has grown and expanded its services to meet the evolving needs of its patients. The clinic has assembled a team of highly skilled dentists, specialists, dental hygienists, and support personnel who work together to deliver top-quality dental care. With their expertise and dedication, Ottawa Dental Clinic has become a go-to destination for individuals seeking exceptional dental treatment and personalized care.

Today, Ottawa Dental Clinic stands as a leading dental practice in Ottawa, known for its state-of-the-art facilities, advanced dental technologies, and a patient-centered approach. The clinic takes pride in its commitment to staying at the forefront of dental advancements and continuously improving its services. With a focus on preventive care, patient education, and a warm and welcoming environment, Ottawa Dental Clinic strives to make every patient's dental experience comfortable, convenient, and rewarding.

Our Team Members

Dr. James Rodriguez

Dr. James Rodriguez is the founder and CEO of Ottawa Dental Clinic. He is a graduate of the University of Toronto and has over 15 years of experience in the dental field. Dr. Rodriguez is a passionate advocate for patient care and is committed to providing the highest quality dental services to all of his patients. He is a respected member of the dental community and is known for his dedication and expertise.

Dr. Bradley Williams

Dr. Bradley Williams, a graduate from McGill University, brings over 5 years of experience to our team, specializing in advanced dental procedures and oral surgery. With a passion for patient care and a commitment to excellence, Dr. Williams ensures each patient receives personalized and compassionate treatment. His expertise and dedication make him a valued member of our dental clinic, trusted by patients and colleagues alike.

Dr. Natalie Smith

Dr. Natalie Smith is a graduate of the University of Ottawa and has been practicing dentistry for over 10 years. She is passionate about providing exceptional dental care to all of her patients. Dr. Smith is known for her gentle touch and friendly demeanor, making her a favourite among patients of all ages. She is committed to providing the highest quality dental services in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

Chloe Johnson

Chloe Johnson is a nurse that works at the dental care clinic and assists the dentists during various procedures. With her expertise and caring nature, Chloe ensures that patients feel comfortable and well taken care of during their dental visits. She plays a crucial role in maintaining a smooth workflow at the clinic and contributes to the overall success of the dental team. Chloe's dedication to patient care and her ability to work closely with the dentists make her an invaluable member of the team.
